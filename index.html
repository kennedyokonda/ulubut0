About Us

We are the
Real change enthusiats

Transparency is our commitment to openness and clarity. We provide clear and honest information about our actions, decisions, and financial matters.y maintaining the highest moral standards, we build trust and credibility, ensuring the integrity of our organization and the causes we champion.

Equality is the foundation of our vision. We advocate for a society where every individual, regardless of gender, has equal opportunities and rights. By championing equality, we break down barriers, fostering an environment where everyone can thrive and contribute their unique strengths.

VISION

To bring about a society in which women, men, boys, and girls enjoy the same opportunities, rights, and obligations in all spheres of life without compromising nature.

MISSION

Empowering communities through education, advocacy, and collaboration, UGE strives to create an inclusive society where individuals of all genders enjoy equal opportunities, rights, and responsibilities. We work passionately to foster gender equality, environmental and social justice, ensuring no one is left behind.

CORE VALUES

Volunteerism, integrity, Transparency, Accountability, Equality, Innovation and Team work

Projects

Gender Equality and Equity

Valuing Our Inclusive Community Experience (VOICE)

VOICE project empowers boys and girls to be ambassadors of change engages girls and boys to collaborate, learn, and grow together, embodying the spirit of inclusivity and equality that Ulubuto Gender Equity advocates for.

Gender-Based Violence (GBV)

Through educational plays and discussions, girls and boys address the issue of GBV. They learn about the importance of consent, empathy, and bystander intervention. Interactive sessions empower them to recognize signs of violence and stand together against any form of abuse

Gender Equality and Equity

Girls and boys engage in discussions that challenge gender stereotypes. They share stories, promoting mutual understanding and respect. Activities include workshops focusing on gender equality, where boys and girls learn about each other's experiences, fostering empathy and cooperation.

Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR)

Girls and boys attend workshops that provide comprehensive information about reproductive health, consent, and gender-sensitive healthcare. They learn to respect each other's boundaries and rights, fostering a safe and supportive environment for all.
Climate Change

Innovative Climate Resilience and Women Empowerment Hub

i-CREH aim at creating a transformative model for sustainable, climate-smart villages in Central Province. By integrating climate change adaptation, food security, gender empowerment, and youth engagement, i-CREH aims to build resilient communities capable of withstanding climate change challenges.
