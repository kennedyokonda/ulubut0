Ulubuto Gender Equity Projects

Valuing Our Inclusive Community Experience(VOICE)

VOICE project empowers boys and girls to be ambassadors of change engages girls and boys to collaborate, learn, and grow together, embodying the spirit of inclusivity and equality that Ulubuto Gender Equity advocates for.

GET MORE INFO

Gender Equality and Equity

Girls and boys engage in discussions that challenge gender stereotypes. They share stories, promoting mutual understanding and respect. Activities include workshops focusing on gender equality, where boys and girls learn about each other's experiences, fostering empathy and cooperation.

GET MORE INFO

Gender-Based Violence (GBV)

Through educational plays and discussions, girls and boys address the issue of GBV. They learn about the importance of consent, empathy, and bystander intervention. Interactive sessions empower them to recognize signs of violence and stand together against any form of abuse.
