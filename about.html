About Us

Brief History of Ulubuto Gender Equality

Ulubuto was formed out of the need for gender equity in Zambia and beyond by a group of people with vast experience and knowledge on the subject.The fight for gender balance requires the involvement of all the stakeholders especially boys,men,women and the disabled to move it to the next and bring about equity.The organization was formerly registered in the Early 2023

Brief Introduction to Ulubuto Gender Equality

To bring about a society in which women, men, boys, and girls enjoy the same opportunities, rights, and obligations in all spheres of life without compromising nature.

Climate change affects people of all genders, races, and backgrounds. At Ulubuto Gender Equity, we advocate for environmental sustainability with a focus on inclusivity. By involving men, women, boys, girls and persons with disabilities in our eco-friendly initiatives, we create a united front against climate change. Through education and collaborative efforts, we empower everyone to become stewards of our planet, ensuring a sustainable future for all.

At Ulubuto Gender Equity, we are reshaping the conversation around gender equality. We recognize that achieving true equity means involving everyone, regardless of their gender. Our focus is not just on empowering women and girls but also engaging boys and men as allies in this crucial journey. By breaking down stereotypes and encouraging open dialogue, we create a society where everyone's strengths are celebrated, and everyone has equal opportunities to thrive.
